Chapter 6: Implied Licenses and Patent Exhaustion

Form 6.04A—Disclaimer of Implied License Based on Existing License Agreement

Notwithstanding all other provisions of this Agreement and the negotiations that led to this Agreement, the parties agree that no right or license, whether expressly or by implication, is granted under any patents other than the Licensed Patents including, but not limited to, under any of the following patents: [Specify Excluded Patents]. Licensor is free to assert infringement claims based on such other patents against Licensee with respect to Licensee’s practice of Licensed Patents and any such assertion will not be considered to be a derogation of Licensee’s rights under any of the Licensed Patents.

Form 6.04B—Definition of Licensed Product

“Licensed Product” means any and all products the manufacture, use,[1] sale, offer for sale or import of which, but for the license granted in this Agreement, would infringe any claim of the Licensed Patent.

Form 6.05—Limited License Pursuant to Sale

Purchase of this machine from Met-Coil provides the purchaser with a limited license to practice Met-Coil’s U.S. Patent No. ______ to make metal ducts using corner pieces purchased from Met-Coil or Authorized Licensees of Met-Coil. A list of Authorized Licensees is available from Met-Coil on request. No license under any Met-Coil patent is transferred to Licensee for making metal ducts using corner pieces obtained from any party other than Met-Coil or an Authorized Licensee.

Form 6.06A—Authority to Sell Conditioned on Royalty Payment

The sale or other disposition of a Licensed Product under this Agreement will not be considered authorized until such time as all applicable royalties due under this Agreement for such Licensed Product have been paid to Licensor by Licensee.

Form 6.06B—Grant Clause for Component of Patented Product

Licensor hereby grants to Licensee a nonexclusive license to make or import Licensed Components and to offer for sale and sell such Licensed Components solely to entities who are licensed by Licensor at the time of such sale or offer for sale.

Form 6.07—Grant Clause for Component of Patented Product

Licensor hereby grants to Licensee a nonexclusive license to make or import Licensed Components and to offer for sale and sell such Licensed Components solely for use in Patented Systems sold by or for Licensor.

Form 6.08—Provision Imposing Contractual Restriction on Licensee’s Customers

In consideration for the license granted herein, Licensee will obtain from each third party purchaser of a Licensed Product a written agreement signed by the third party that includes the following paragraph:

     [The third party] agrees that its purchase of the [Licensed Product] from [Licensee] does not give [the third party] any right or license to use the [Licensed Product] in any product or method claimed in a patent owned by [Licensor]. [The third party] understands that, as of the date of this Agreement, there is no lawful use of the [Licensed Product] without a license under certain patents owned by [Licensor]. [The third party] further understands that upon application [Licensor] will grant a license to [the third party] on standard terms. [The third party] hereby agrees not to use the [Licensed Product] for any purpose without the necessary license. [The third party] may resell the [Licensed Product] in the form acquired from [Licensee] provided [the third party] first obtains from any such purchaser a written agreement signed by such purchaser that includes this provision. [The third party] will promptly deliver to [Licensee] the original of any such written agreement by a purchaser. [The third party] agrees that this contract provision is for the benefit of the [Licensor].

Licensee will deliver to Licensor the original of any such agreement obtained by Licensee, directly or indirectly, from any third party. The Licensee agrees that compliance with this Section ___ is material to the agreement and that Licensor may terminate this Agreement pursuant to Section ___ for any failure to comply with the terms hereof.

Form 6.09—Notice Obligation Imposed on Territorially Restricted Licensee

     Prior to purchase, Licensee agrees to inform all purchasers of Licensed Products that they will not have any right to export such Licensed Products to or use and resell such Licensed Products in countries outside the Licensed Territory where Licensor owns patents. Accordingly, Licensee will place prominently on all sales brochures and sales documents a notice in the following form:

     Purchase of [commercial name for Licensed Products] provides the purchaser with a limited license to use and resell the purchased [commercial name for Licensed Products] only in [countries of the Licensed Territory]. Purchaser has no right or license under applicable patents to use or re-sell the purchased [commercial name for Licensed Products] outside such countries.


[1]But see Zenith Elecs. Corp. v. PDI Commc’ns Sys., 522 F.3d 1348, 86 USPQ2d 1513 (Fed. Cir. 2008).
