Form B3—Assignment of an Invention, With Ensuing Patent Rights—by the Inventor to a Corporation[1]

In consideration of one dollar ($1.00) and other good and valuable consideration, of which receipt is acknowledged, I ________________, residing at ____________________, sell and assign to ABC Company, a Delaware corporation, the entire right, title, and interest in and to the _________, invented by me as described in the application for U.S. patent executed by me concurrently herewith, and any and all applications for patent and patents in any and all countries, including all divisions, continuations, reissues, and extensions thereof and all rights of priority resulting from the filing of said U.S. application. I further authorize and request any official whose duty it is to issue patents to issue any patent on said invention or resulting therefrom to the ABC Company, or its successors, assigns, or nominees, and I agree that on request and without further consideration, but at the expense of the ABC Company, I will communicate to ABC Company or its representatives or nominees any facts known to me respecting said improvements and testify in any legal proceedings, sign all lawful papers, execute all divisional, continuing, and reissue applications, make all rightful oaths, and generally aid the ABC Company, its successors, assigns, and nominees to obtain and enforce proper patent protection for said invention in all countries.

_____________________________, 20__.   ___________________________________

(Acknowledgment)

Form B4—Agreement of Assignment by One Company to Another (With Separate Recordable Assignment)

ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

IN THIS AGREEMENT, made and entered into as of the __________ day of ______________________, 20__ (Effective Date), ABC Company, an Illinois corporation having offices at _____________ (ABC), and XYZ Corporation, a California corporation having offices at _____________ (XYZ), agree as follows:

1. ABC owns all right, title, and interest in and to U.S. Patent No. 7,654,123 (the ‘123 Patent), the invention disclosed and claimed therein, and all worldwide patents and applications that rely on the ‘123 Patent for priority (the Patents).

2. XYZ desires to acquire and ABC is willing to assign to XYZ all of ABC’s right, title, and interest in and to the Patents and any inventions disclosed and claimed therein.

3. ABC hereby transfers, grants, conveys, assigns, and relinquishes exclusively to XYZ all of ABC’s right, title, and interest in and to the Patents, the inventions claimed therein, and all accrued causes of action for damages for infringement thereof.[2]

4. In consideration, on the Effective Date, XYZ will pay to ABC the sum of _________ dollars ($__________) by certified check or wire transfer, as specified by ABC.

5. ABC will execute and deliver to XYZ the Assignment in Attachment A hereto, and, from time to time after the date hereof upon the request of XYZ, such further conveyance instruments as may be necessary or desirable to evidence more fully the transfer of ownership of all the Patents to XYZ, or the original ownership of all the Patents on the part of ABC, to the fullest extent possible. ABC further agrees to provide testimony in connection with any proceeding affecting the right, title, interest, or benefit of XYZ in and to the Patents and to perform any other acts deemed reasonably necessary to carry out the intent of this Agreement. XYZ will reimburse ABC for any and all costs reasonably incurred by ABC in performance under this paragraph.

6. In furtherance of this Agreement, ABC hereby acknowledges that, from the Effective Date forward, XYZ has succeeded to all of ABC’s right, title, and standing to receive all rights and benefits pertaining to the Patents, institute and prosecute all suits and proceedings, and take all actions that XYZ, in its sole discretion, may deem necessary or proper to collect, assert, or enforce any claim, right, or title of any kind under any and all of the Patents, whether arising before or after the Effective Date, defend and compromise any and all such actions, suits, or proceedings relating to such transferred and assigned rights, title, interest, and benefits, and do all other such acts and things in relation thereto as XYZ, in its sole discretion, deems advisable.

7. ABC represents and warrants that no consents of any other parties are necessary or appropriate under any agreements concerning any of the Patents in order for the transfer and assignment of any of the Patents under this Agreement to be legally effective.

8. ABC represents and warrants that, to the best of ABC’s knowledge, upon consummation of this Agreement, XYZ shall have good and marketable title to the Patents, free and clear of any and all liens, mortgages, encumbrances, pledges, security interests, licenses, or charges of any nature whatsoever.

9. This Agreement shall inure to the benefit of, and be binding on, the parties hereto together with their respective legal representatives, successors, and assigns.

10. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York (excluding conflicts of law rules) and of the United States.

11. This Agreement merges and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous agreements, assurances, representations, and communications between or among the parties hereto concerning the matters set forth herein.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have executed this Agreement under seal the day and year first above written.

ABC Company

ATTACHMENT A

ASSIGNMENT

WHEREAS, ABC Company, an Illinois corporation having offices at ________________ (ABC), owns, by assignment, all right, title, and interest in U.S. Patent No. _________, entitled _______________, and any invention claimed therein; and

XYZ Corporation, a California corporation having offices at _______________________ (XYZ), desires to own ABC’s entire right, title, and interest in and to the invention, in all countries throughout the world, and in and to U.S. Letters Patent No. ________.

NOW THEREFORE, be it known that, for good and valuable consideration, receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, ABC hereby sells, assigns, transfers, and sets over to XYZ, its lawful successors and assigns, ABC’s entire right, title, and interest in and to U.S. Patent No. _________, the invention claimed therein, any other patent application directed to the invention, and all Letters Patent of the United States that may be granted thereon, and all reissues, reexaminations, and extensions thereof; and all rights to claim priority on the basis of such application, and all applications for Letters Patent that may be filed for the invention in any foreign country and all Letters Patent that may be granted on the invention in any foreign country, and all extensions, renewals, and reissues thereof; and ABC hereby authorizes and requests the Commissioner of Patents and Trademarks of the United States and any official of any foreign country whose duty it is to issue patents on applications as described above, to issue all Letters Patent for this invention to XYZ, its successors and assigns, in accordance with the terms of this Assignment;

AND, ABC HEREBY further covenants that ABC has the full right to convey the interest assigned by this Assignment, ABC will take all action and execute all documents necessary to perfect the interest assigned hereby, and ABC has not executed and will not execute any agreement in conflict with this Assignment;

AND, ABC HEREBY further covenants and agrees that ABC, through its officers and employees, will, without further consideration, communicate with XYZ, its successors and assigns, any facts known to ABC and its officers and employees respecting the invention and testify in any legal proceeding, sign all lawful papers when called upon to do so, execute and deliver all papers that may be necessary or desirable to perfect the title to the invention in said XYZ, its successors and assigns, execute all divisional, continuation, and reissue applications, make all rightful oaths, and generally do everything possible to aid XYZ, its successors and assigns, to obtain and enforce proper patent protection for the invention in the United States and any foreign country, it being understood that any expense incident to the execution of such papers shall be borne by XYZ, its successors and assigns.

IN TESTIMONY WHEREOF, each party has caused its authorized representative to execute this Assignment.

ABC Company
