Chapter 22: Execution of Agreements: Problems of Authority and Proof

Form 22.00A—Assent

John P. Jones, whose address is ______________, warrants that he is the owner of the entire right, title, and interest in and to the Licensed Patent, identified as such in the Agreement to which this Assent is appended, including the right to recover for and to grant releases for past infringement, and that he has granted to Licensor named in the Agreement an exclusive license under the Licensed Patent with the right to grant sublicenses and to recover for and to release past infringement, which license continues in force on the date when he executes this Assent. The said John P. Jones hereby acknowledges that Licensor has the right and license to grant the rights, releases, and sublicenses granted to Licensee in the Agreement, and to the extent that any of his rights are or may be affected, agrees to be bound by the terms and conditions of the said Agreement, and further agrees that he will look to Licensor, and not to Licensee, for any and all royalty payments and other remunerations, if any, that are due or that might become due him by virtue of the licenses, rights, and releases granted to Licensee under the Agreement. He further agrees that if the exclusive license that he has granted to Licensor and that is now in effect and under the authority of which the present Agreement has been entered into between Licensor and Licensee is canceled or terminated for whatever reason prior to the end of term of the present Agreement, the rights, licenses, and releases granted under the present Agreement will continue in full force and effect, the same as if he, John P. Jones, had been the original Licensor, provided the Licensee agrees in writing to thereafter render to John P. Jones all obligations that the present Agreement then requires Licensee to render to Licensor. He further agrees that the terms, conditions, and obligations of this Assent will be binding on himself, his heirs, assigns, and legal representatives.

ASSENT DATED:

____________________________
John P. Jones

____________________________
Witness

Form 22.00B—Quiet Enjoyment

Licensor hereby covenants that Licensee, its Affiliates, sublicensees, and customers have the right to quiet enjoyment of the Licensed Patents and Licensed Know-How in accordance with the terms of this Agreement. In the event of any assertion against Licensee, its Affiliates, sublicensees, or customers by any Third Party of rights under any Licensed Patent or Licensed Know-How that are inconsistent with rights granted Licensee in this Agreement, Licensor will defend and indemnify Licensee, its Affiliates, sublicensees, and customers against such assertion and will obtain for Licensee from such Third Party rights under the Licensed Patents and Licensed Know-How to the extent necessary for such quiet enjoyment.

Form 22.01A—Execution Paragraph

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, each of the parties hereto has caused this Agreement to be executed in duplicate originals by its duly authorized officers or representatives.

Form 22.01B—Execution Paragraph (Alternate)

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, each of the parties hereto has caused its corporate seal to be affixed and duly attested and this Agreement to be signed by its authorized representative.

Form 22.01C—Execution Paragraph (Alternate for Geographically Dispersed Parties)

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, each of the parties hereto has caused this Agreement to be executed by its duly authorized officers or representatives in counterpart duplicate originals signed by facsimile.

Form 22.01D—Execution Paragraph (Individual)

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, XYZ Corporation has caused its corporate seal to be affixed and duly attested and this Agreement to be signed by its authorized representative, and Jane B. Smith has signed the Agreement in her own behalf.

Form 22.01E—Execution Paragraph (Partnership)

and (name of partnership) has caused this Agreement to be signed by ____________________________, a general partner.
