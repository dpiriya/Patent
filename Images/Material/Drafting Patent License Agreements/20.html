Chapter 20: Common Provisions of Consequence

Form 20.00A—Choice of Law Provision 1

This Agreement will be construed, interpreted, and applied in accordance with the laws of the State of _________.

Form 20.00B—Choice of Law Provision 2

This Agreement will be governed in all respects by the laws of the State of _________, except that its conflict of law rules shall not apply.

Form 20.00C—Choice of Law Provision 3

This Agreement will be interpreted and construed, and the legal relations created herein will be determined, in accordance with the laws of the State of _________ (excluding conflicts of laws) and of the United States.

Form 20.00D—Choice of Law Provision 4

The Parties, recognizing that the laws of other jurisdictions might have a closer connection to this Agreement or to the Parties, have decided that this Agreement will be interpreted and construed, and the legal relations created herein will be determined, in accordance with the laws of the State of _________ (excluding conflicts of laws) and of the United States.

Form 20.00E—Opt Out of UCITA

The Uniform Computer Information Transaction Act, as adopted by any state, will not govern any aspect of this Agreement or actions taken relating to this Agreement, the parties having decided to opt out of automatic coverage. If (choice of law state) should enact UCITA during the term of this Agreement, the substantive contract law of (choice of law state) other than UCITA will be applied to this Agreement and any actions taken relating to this Agreement.

Form 20.00F—Choice of Law and Venue Provision

This Agreement, including all matters of construction, validity, and performance, will be governed by and construed and enforced in accordance with the laws of the State of _________, as applied to contracts made, executed, and to be fully performed in such state by citizens of such state, without regard to its conflict of law rules. The parties agree that the exclusive[1] jurisdiction and venue for any action brought between the parties arising under this Agreement will be the state and federal courts sitting in _________, and each of the parties hereby agrees and submits itself to the exclusive jurisdiction and venue of such courts for such purpose.

Form 20.00G—Alternative Venue Provision 1

The parties hereto agree that the exclusive jurisdiction and venue for any action arising under this Agreement if brought by Licensor will be the state and federal courts sitting in [Licensee’s state], and if brought by Licensee will be the state and federal courts sitting in [Licensor’s state], and each of the parties hereby agrees and submits itself to the exclusive jurisdiction and venue of such courts for such purpose.

Form 20.00H—Alternative Venue Provision 2

The parties agree that the exclusive jurisdiction and venue for any action arising under this Agreement will be the state and federal courts sitting in _________, and each of the parties hereby agrees and submits itself to the exclusive jurisdiction and venue of such courts for such purpose. This provision will apply to actions pertaining to the validity of Licensed Patents but will not apply to reexamination or reissue proceedings in the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office.

Form 20.01A—Notice Provision 1

Any notice, report, request, or statement provided for in this Agreement will be deemed effective when sent by certified or registered mail addressed to the party for whom intended at the address given at the outset of this Agreement or at such changed address as the party has specified by written notice.

Either party may change its effective address by giving thirty (30) days’ notice of the new address in the manner provided in paragraph 1, above.

Form 20.02A—Marking Provision 1

Licensee will mark all Licensed Products sold or otherwise disposed of by it under the license granted in this Agreement with the word “Patent” and the number of the Licensed Patent.

Form 20.02B—Marking Provision 2

Licensee will mark all Licensed Products sold by it under the license granted with the word “Patent” or “Patents” and the number or numbers of the Licensed Patent or Licensed Patents applicable thereto.

Form 20.02C—Marking Provision 3

Licensee will comply with the reasonable requirements of Licensor with respect to the marking of articles sold under the license herein granted with the word “Patent,” followed by the number or numbers of the patent or patents applicable thereto under which a license is granted hereunder.

Form 20.02D—Marking Provision 4

Licensee will mark any products made using a process covered by any Licensed Patent with the number of each such patent and, with respect to Licensed Patents, will respond to any requests for disclosure under 35 U.S.C. §287(b)(4)(B) by notifying Licensor of the request for disclosure.

Form 20.03A—Force Majeure Clause

Neither party will be responsible or liable to the other party for nonperformance or delay in performance of any terms or conditions of this Agreement due to acts or occurrences beyond the control of the nonperforming or delayed party, including, but not limited to, acts of God, acts of government, terrorism, wars, riots, strikes or other labor disputes, shortages of labor or materials, fires, and floods, provided the nonperforming or delayed party provides to the other party written notice of the existence of and the reason for such nonperformance or delay.

Form 20.03B—Force Majeure Clause Protection in Exclusive License

Any nonperformance or delay of Licensee subject to this paragraph that is in excess of one hundred eighty (180) days will constitute cause for termination by Licensor of this Agreement upon written notice to Licensee.

Form 20.03C—Force Majeure Clause Exception for Fixed Payments

Nothing in this paragraph excuses timely payment by Licensee of the amounts due pursuant to paragraph ___ of this Agreement.

Form 20.04—Severability Provision

Should any part or provision of this Agreement be held unenforceable or in conflict with the law of any jurisdiction, the validity of the remaining parts or provisions will not be affected by such holding. In the event a part or provision of this Agreement held unenforceable or in conflict with law affects consideration to either party, the parties agree to negotiate in good faith amendment of such part or provision in a manner consistent with the intention of the parties as expressed in this Agreement.

Form 20.05—Course of Conduct Disclaimer

No course of dealing, course of performance, or usage of trade may be considered in the interpretation or enforcement of this agreement. Both parties waive any right they may have to introduce any such evidence.

Form 20.06—Nonwaiver Provision

No failure or delay on the part of either Party in the exercise of any right, power, or privilege hereunder will operate as a waiver thereof. No single or partial exercise of any right, power, or privilege hereunder will operate as a waiver thereof or of any other right, power, or privilege. The waiver by either Party of a breach of any provision of this Agreement may be effected only by a writing signed by the waiving Party and will not operate or be construed as a waiver of any other or subsequent breach hereunder.

Form 20.07A—Integration Provision

This instrument contains the entire and only agreement between the parties and supersedes all preexisting agreements between them respecting its subject matter. Any representation, promise, or condition in connection with such subject matter that is not incorporated in this Agreement will not be binding on either party. No modification, renewal, extension, or waiver, and (except as provided in Articles ___ and ___ hereof) no termination of this Agreement or any of its provisions, will be binding on the party against whom enforcement of such modification, renewal, extension, waiver, or termination is sought, unless made in writing and signed on behalf of such party by one of its executive officers. As used herein, the word “termination” includes any and all means of bringing to an end prior to its expiration by its own terms this Agreement, or any provision thereof, whether by release, discharge, abandonment, or otherwise.

Form 20.07B—Express Negative Covenant in Field of Use License

Licensee acknowledges and agrees that the rights granted to it under this Agreement are limited to the licenses granted in paragraphs ___ and ___ hereof. Licensee acknowledges that the grant of those rights is conditioned on its agreement to refrain from using Licensed Patents or Licensed Technology outside the Licensed Field and the Licensed Territory and that any such activity by Licensee will be a material breach of this Agreement.

Form 20.08A—Government Approval Provision 1

If government approval of this agreement is required, Licensee agrees to use reasonable, good faith efforts to obtain, within six (6) months after the Effective Date, approval of this agreement from all relevant government agencies or authorities having jurisdiction.

Form 20.08B—Government Approval Provision 2

This Agreement will be effective as of the Effective Date provided it has been formally approved prior to that date by the competent authorities of the Federal Trade Commission of the United States. Otherwise, it shall become effective as of the date of such approval. If such approval has not been obtained by January XX, 20__, this Agreement shall become void and of no effect.


[1]See SW Intelecom, Inc. v. Hotel Networks Corp., 997 S.W.2d 322, 325 (Tex. App. 1999) (allowing the Texas court to retain jurisdiction over a contract dispute despite a contract provision providing that both parties agreed to jurisdiction for disputes in Minnesota, and noting that the provision did not provide for exclusive jurisdiction in Minnesota).
