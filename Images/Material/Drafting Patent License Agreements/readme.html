Bloomberg BNA License Agreement
 

The CD-ROM provided with this Seventh Edition provides access to a set of form paragraphs that can be incorporated, with careful editing, into a license agreement being prepared by the reader. The form paragraphs are indexed to the text which provides the rationale and legal support concerning whether a particular form paragraph is appropriate to accomplish the drafter’s specific goals. The computer disk also includes the form agreements contained in the appendices. The changes to the forms and samples provided reflect legal developments encountered since the last edition. As with all legal documents, the form and content of license agreements reflect the legal concepts involved in transfer of rights to patents and know-how.

The changes to the forms and samples provided reflect legal developments encountered since the last edition. In view of the dynamic nature of the law, particularly patent law, the vitality of the forms, samples and other contents of this CD-ROM should be updated and confirmed before taking any action.
