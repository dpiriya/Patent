Chapter 5: Specific Types of Licenses

Form 5.03A—Right to Grant Sublicenses

Licensor hereby grants to Licensee the right to grant sublicenses in accordance with the terms of this Agreement. Licensee will require each sublicensee to pay at least the royalties specified in Section ___ of this Agreement and to keep records and render reports as required in Sections ___. Licensee may, for its own benefit, extract other consideration, including other payments, from sublicensees. Licensee may not grant sublicensees the right to grant sublicenses or the right to enforce any Licensed Patent. All sublicenses will be in writing and, except as expressly provided in this Section ___, will have terms and conditions no less restrictive than those in this Agreement. Licensee will include in each sublicense agreement a provision that identifies Licensor as an intended beneficiary thereof. Licensee will deliver to Licensor a true copy of each sublicense within thirty (30) days after it is executed.

Form 5.03B—Post-Termination Sublicense Rights

Upon termination of this Agreement for any reason prior to expiration pursuant to Section ___, each authorized sublicense granted by Licensee will terminate unless, within sixty (60) days after the date of termination of this Agreement, the sublicensee delivers to Licensor a written agreement to render to Licensor any performance, including payment of money, due from the sublicensee pursuant to the sublicense agreement as of and after the date of termination of this Agreement. Licensee will include this option in each sublicense, and Licensee will provide each sublicensee written notice of the date of termination of this Agreement within ten (10) days thereafter.

Form 5.03C—Post-Termination Sublicense Rights

Effective on the date of termination of this Agreement for any reason prior to expiration pursuant to Section ___, Licensee hereby assigns to Licensor each authorized sublicense agreement that is in effect on the date of termination. Licensee will notify, in writing, each sublicensee of such assignment within ten days after the date of termination of this Agreement. Licensee will include notification of this provision in each sublicense.

Form 5.03D—Early Termination of Sublicenses

Each sublicense will terminate upon termination of this Agreement, and Licensee will include a statement to that effect in each sublicense.

Form 5.03E—No Bundling of Sublicensed Rights

An agreement granting a sublicense will not include the grant of rights under property owned by the Licensee or any third party. Licensee agrees that all revenue received by it under any sublicense agreement will be attributable to Licensed Patents and Licensor will be entitled to its share of such revenue in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.
