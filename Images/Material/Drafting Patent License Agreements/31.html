Bloomberg BNA License Agreement
 
Chapter 31: University and Government Licensing

Form 31.00A—Representation Regarding Bayh-Dole Act

Licensor represents that the inventions disclosed and claimed in the Licensed Patent were conceived and reduced to practice with funds from the U.S. Government. Licensor further represents that it has complied with all requirements of the applicable funding agreement with the U.S. Government in order to obtain title to the Licensed Patent. Licensee also represents that it has complied with all statutes and regulations of the U.S. Government concerning the inventions disclosed and claimed in the Licensed Patent in order to obtain title to the Licensed Patent, including those of 35 U.S.C. §§200 et seq. and those of 37 U.S.C. §§401 et seq.

Form 31.00B—Representation Regarding Receipt of Government Funds

Licensor represents that the inventions disclosed and claimed in the Licensed Patent were not conceived or reduced to practice with funds provided by the U.S. Government.
